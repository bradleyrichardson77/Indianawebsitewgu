General information:

Bloomington, Indiana was founded in 1818 by a group of settlers from neighboring states. Today the population stands at 79,000 residents. This urban city is located roughly 52 miles southwest of Indianapolis and 230 miles southeast of Chicago, Illinois in a mostly rural area. Bloomington is a college town host to Indiana University making the city very popular, and rival to the other large college towns in the state. The average income of a Bloomington resident is 21,000 USD which is roughly 14,000 USD less than the state average.
