General information:

Previously home to the Lenape tribe, Indianapolis, Indiana was founded in 1821 platted by Alexander Ralston and Elias Pym Fordham. Today the population stands at 876,384 residents. This urban city is located in the center of the state surrounded by many suburban towns. The average income of an Indianapolis resident is 35,500 USD which is roughly the same as the state average. It is home to 40 institutions of higher learning.

Fun facts:

  • The Indianapolis city flag is a white star set in a red circle which is Centered in a white cross with a blue background.
  • Indianapolis is sometimes referred to as circle-city because of "Monument Circle" downtown. (Life In Indy)
  • Indianapolis is also the home of Wonder Bread sliced bread. (Rogers)
  • The underground railroad ran through the city harbored by the 'Slippery Noodle Inn'. (Brooks)
  • Indianapolis is home to a 3900 acre wildlife park Eagle Creek park
