General information:

Lafayette, Indiana was platted in 1825 by william digby, and incorporated in 1853. Today the population stands at 72,000 residents. This urban city is located roughly 65 miles northwest of Indianapolis and 110 miles southeast of Chicago, Illinois in a mostly rural area. Its twin city West Lafayette is seperated only by the Wabash river and is host to Purdue University making both the cities a collegiate hot spot. the average income of a Lafayette resident is 33,000 USD which is roughly 2000 USD less than the state average.
