General information:

In 1816 Indiana became the 19th state to join the USA. Indiana is a mostly rural state located in the midwest with a current population of 6.8 million residents. The average Indiana residents makes 35,000 USD every year.

Top ranked universities by enrollment in 2024:

Fun facts:

  • Indiana uses the state colors of blue and gold for their flag!
  • The state flower is a peony.
  • Indiana is the first state to have a chapel in its state capitol. (Indiana House Republicans)
  • The state motto, adopted in 1937, is "The Crossroads of America."(IHB)
  • The state seal has been used since 1801.(britannica)
